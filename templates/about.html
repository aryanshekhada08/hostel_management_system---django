About Our Hostel Management System

Building Smart & Secure Hostel Administration

Who We Are

Our Hostel Management System is a modern web-based platform designed to simplify hostel operations including admissions, room allocation, fee management, wallet control, notifications, and complaints handling. It provides a secure and organized way to manage students and hostel resources.

Our Mission

To provide a digital solution that makes hostel management efficient, transparent, and easy for administrators and students.

Our Vision

To transform traditional hostel management into a smart, automated, and secure system using modern web technologies.

Key Features

Admission Management

Digital admission workflow with admin approval.

Wallet & Transactions

Admin-controlled pocket money with email alerts.

Online Fee Tracking

Secure fee payments and invoice generation.

Developed By

This system is developed as an advanced academic project demonstrating real-world ERP-style architecture using Django & Tailwind CSS.
